Why use lime?

Lime mortar is a sustainable, long-lasting and historically-authentic material. But before using it for repointing work, there are a number of things to consider.

What’s the overall context of the building? What type of stone is it constructed from? How exposed is it to the environment?

What materials should be used? Which choice of lime and aggregate is appropriate for this building?

How should I carry out the work? How can I ensure the repointing is successful?

This lime mortar guidance tool is designed to help you identify the right materials and the appropriate best-practice methods to use.
